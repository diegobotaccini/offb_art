OFFB INFO

OFFB (b. Brazil) is a contemporary artist whose practice originates in over two decades of engagement with graffiti and pixação culture across Brazil, Portugal, and Germany. His work translates the expressive urgency of urban mark-making into a distinctive neocubist language, where geometry, rhythm, and color coexist in a refined balance between spontaneity and structure.

Rooted in the visual intensity of the streets yet evolved toward the compositional sophistication of fine art, OFFB’s paintings and murals explore the tension between order and chaos — a dialogue between lived experience and formal abstraction. Now based in Hamburg, he continues to expand his visual vocabulary, positioning his work within the intersection of street culture and contemporary art.
